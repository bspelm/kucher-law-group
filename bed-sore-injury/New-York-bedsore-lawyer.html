Most New York bedsore malpractice claims allege that the care facility failed to frequently move the patient or follow best practices to prevent bedsores. These practices include regular repositioning, ensuring adequate nutrition and hydration, and utilizing pressure-relieving mattresses. A New York bedsore lawyer can argue these points effectively to support your claim.
